Traditional Slovak Honey Cookies

Prelož

Time

24 h + 1 h

Ingredients

  • 1 kg all-purpose flour
  • 25 dag ground wallnuts
  • 0.5 kg powder sugar
  • 30 dag honey
  • 1.5 tsp baking soda
  • 6 tbs (50 g) melted shortening
  • 1 tsp ground cinamon
  • 1 tsp ground cloves (or gingerbread spice)
  • vanilla sugar / extract

Method

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl, wrap in cligfoil and let rest 24 hours in a fridge.
  2. Line baking tray with baking paper. Roll the dough into a 3-4 mm sheet and cut out desired shapes.
  3. Bake for 5 – 7 min. or until golden brown at 230 °C
